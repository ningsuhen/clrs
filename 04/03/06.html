Exercise 4.3.6

Show that the solution to $T(n) = 2T(\lfloor n/2 \rfloor + 17) + n$ is $O(n\lg{n})$

Let's guess $T(n) \le c(n-a)\lg(n-a)$:

$$ \begin{align} T(n) & \le 2c(\lfloor n/2 \rfloor + 17 - a)\lg(\lfloor n/2 \rfloor + 17 - a) + n \\ & \le 2c(n/2 + 1 + 17 - a)\lg(n/2 + 1 + 17 - a) + n \\ & \le c(n + 36 - 2a)\lg\frac{n + 36 - 2a}{2} + n \\ & \le c(n + 36 - 2a)\lg(n + 36 - 2a) - c(n + 36 - 2a) + n & (c > 1, n > n_0 = f(a))\\ & \le c(n + 36 - 2a)\lg(n + 36 - 2a) & (a \ge 36) \\ & \le c(n - a)\lg(n - a) \end{align} $$
